About Me

Hi, my name is Gary. I am a currently a web developer and a graduate student in Information Science, specializing in Human-Computer Interaction, at the University of Michigan.

I was initially a latecomer to the world of tech, having learnt to code right after finishing my undergraduate degree. Over the course of my time away from school, my background in psychology led to become curious about the human facing side of software development. This lead me to return to school in order to learn to bring human-centered principles into development.

Over the course of my two years in the program I learned:

  • practical user-centered research methods such as conducting interviews, usability tests, contextual inquiry
  • designed and developed data visualization programs that tell meaningful narratives
  • ideated, designed, and prototyped interfaces for new apps
  • collected and manipulated data from the web to further project goals

I am graduating in April 2018 and currently seeking fulltime opportunities in UI and web development. Feel free to contact me at gcheung@nullumich.edu
